    ⛔ No Trophies Or Achievements

    The moment you activate any cheat in GTA 6, trophies (PS5) and achievements (Xbox/PC) are disabled for that entire play session. Save before using cheats and keep a clean save file if going for 100% or platinum.

    📱 Primary Input — In-Game Phone

    GTA 6 is expected to use the in-game smartphone as the primary cheat input — dial the number, activate the code. Confirmed by the "Cheat Activated" UI string found in leaked game files.

    🎮 Button Combinations Also Work

    Standard controller combos (PS5 and Xbox) will work during gameplay — not from the pause menu. Enter the sequence quickly and in exact order. A screen flash confirms activation.

    💻 PC Console Command

    Press the tilde (~) key during active gameplay to open the console. Type the command and press Enter. The console closes automatically after activation.

    🔄 Cheats Toggle On / Off

    Entering the same cheat twice deactivates it (if applicable). Invincibility has a timer. Wanted level cheats are one-directional per entry. Moon gravity and slow motion are stackable.

    🚫 Online? Forget It.

    Cheat codes are strictly single-player story mode only. Since GTA IV, Rockstar has fully disabled cheats online. Using exploits or modified cheats in GTA 6 Online risks a permanent account ban.

    🎯 Missions Compatibility

    In GTA V, many cheats were restricted to free roam and couldn't be activated during missions. GTA 6's mission cheat compatibility is unknown — Hub+ will report this from day one gameplay.

    What's New & Confirmed

    All Confirmed Features

    First Female GTA Protagonist

    Lucia Caminos is the first playable female lead in mainline GTA — a franchise milestone. Her father taught her fighting skills; she originally lived in Liberty City. The game opens with her prison release.

    Jason Duval — Ex-Military

    Former Army with a possible paratrooper background (confirmed shoulder tattoo, Trailer 2). Post-service he ended up doing debt collection for a criminal named Brian in the Leonida Keys.

    Real-Time Character Switching

    Three modes: Solo Jason, Solo Lucia, and dual protagonist control. Switching happens mid-mission during heists — with different outcomes depending on who you control at key moments.

    In-Game Social Network

    A TikTok/Instagram-style feed in the in-game phone. Browse viral videos, track influencers, discover secret side missions — some influencers are confirmed main story NPCs.

    Next-Gen Combat Overhaul

    Confirmed: prone crawling, zip ties to restrain NPCs, human shields, revamped 6-star wanted system. Voice lines adapt dynamically to mission stress and player choices.

    200+ Vehicles — Full Interiors

    Cars, motorcycles, helicopters, planes, and boats — all with fully modeled interactive interiors. Wristwatches show real in-game time. Beer bottles have fluid physics.

    Dynamic Weather — Hurricanes

    Tropical storms can temporarily alter the map and change gameplay. Hurricanes affect NPC behaviour, road conditions, and mission availability — a first for the series at this scale.

    Smartest NPC AI In GTA History

    NPCs have individual daily routines generating emergent random events. They respond to time of day, weather, and player reputation. Rockstar patents describe adaptive dialogue profiles per NPC.

    Unprecedented Visuals

    Ray Traced Global Illumination, RT Reflections, strand-based hair, dynamic clothing, sweat systems, growing facial hair, parallax-mapped building interiors, and volumetric cloud systems.

    700+ Shops & Enterable Locations

    Gameroll (credible track record) claims 700 shops. Amazon Brazil confirmed "interactive establishments" throughout Leonida. Rockstar patents from 2020–2024 describe procedural interior generation.

    Side Activities Confirmed

    Via the Dec 2025 GTA Online crossover: fishing, hunting, mini-golf, scuba diving, kayaking, MMA fighting, pet customisation, and a workout/fitness system are all confirmed.

    GTA Online 2.0 — Post-Launch

    Launches single-player only. Online mode confirmed post-launch with fresh economy, no GTA V transfers. Rockstar's FiveM acquisition suggests structured roleplay tools. Veteran loyalty bonuses expected.
